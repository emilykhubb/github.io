# Methodology (Summary)

## Definitions
- Quick resale (≤2 years): property sold again within 2 years of previous sale date
- Quick resale (≤5 years): property sold again within 5 years of previous sale date
- First-to-last % change: percent change between a property’s first recorded sale and its last recorded sale

## Notes
- Sales history can include multiple transactions per property
- Results are aggregated at the postcode-area level for comparison
