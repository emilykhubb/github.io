# Boss Evaluation Rubric (Q1–Q5)

## Questions

| Category | Q1–Q5 | Question |
|---|---|---|
| Communication | Q1 | How effectively did the designer communicate with the client and maintain a positive relationship throughout the project? |
| Design | Q2 | How strong was the overall design, attention to detail, and execution of the project? |
| Organization | Q3 | How well did the designer stay organized, manage timelines, and keep the project on track? |
| Problem Solving | Q4 | How effectively did the designer handle challenges, changes, or unexpected issues during the project? |
| Professionalism | Q5 | How reliable and professional was the designer (ownership of work, follow-through, accountability)? |

---

## Scoring Definitions (1–5)

| Score | Definition |
|---|---|
| 1 | Poor – Performance did not meet expectations and required significant correction or intervention. |
| 2 | Below Average – Performance occasionally met expectations but showed noticeable inconsistencies and required guidance. |
| 3 | Meets Expectations – Performance met expectations with reliable and consistent execution of responsibilities. |
| 4 | Above Average – Performance exceeded expectations with strong execution, initiative, and attention to detail. |
| 5 | Excellent – Performance was exceptional, consistently exceeding expectations with outstanding ownership and execution. |
